They are so cute!

Giant pandas grow to between 1.2m and 1.5m, and weigh between 75kg and 135kg. Scientists are not sure how long pandas live in the wild, but in captivity they live to be around 30 years old Pandas do not hibernate, and this is unlike most other bears. When winter approaches, they head lower down their mountain homes to warmer temperatures, where they continue to eat away on bamboo just like usual! Sadly, these cute creatures are endangered. It's been estimated that around 1,000 remain in the wild. We need to do eveyrthing we can to protect them.
