What is it

It's a chain of convience stores that also serve food

Started in Pennsylvania

Let's get into it

Sheetz was founded by Bob Sheetz in 1952 in Altoona, Pennsylvania.

It was founded on the basis of efficiency and freshness.

Sheetz prides itself on its loyalty to its costomers. Since day 1, Sheetz has evolved and rebranded to fit customers' demands. To emphasize this mission, on Sheetz website it states:
Our vision is to create the business that will put Sheetz, as we know it today, out of business. That's what keeps us on top and keeps us focused on ways to make our business even more sucessful in the future.

A Common Misconception

Many think of Sheetz as just a gas station, but it is way more than that. Many loyal Sheetz customers find it irritating when referring to Sheetz as just a gas station, because it takes away from the delicious meals they serve.
