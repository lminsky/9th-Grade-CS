Wear Or Tear These Rachel Green Looks And We'll Reveal Your Style IQ

  • by Bri Thomas
  • – on Feb 09th
  • in Lifestyle

One of the most stylish television characters of all time is Rachel Green. On “Friends” she was seen in everything from an apron and a wedding dress to pajamas, little black dresses, and plaid skirts. She was young and attractive, living out her life in New York and working her way into the fashion industry, and after 236 episodes that featured her doing all of this, fans were able to see her in some super fashionable outfits and in some not-so-great looks, too. It was a different time, and she was a trendsetter who sometimes took fashion risks, so keep that in mind as you take this quiz!

You will see 35 of Rachel Green’s looks below, and for each one, you will tell us if you would wear or tear them. Was her original wedding dress your style? Did you like her knee-high socks? Do you still dream about her overalls, her pink fur coat or her Princess Leia outfit? Have you ever copied her clothing items and/or her haircut at any point over the last two decades?

See all of these looks here, rate them all now, and then find out the truth: Is your style IQ super high or pretty horrible?
