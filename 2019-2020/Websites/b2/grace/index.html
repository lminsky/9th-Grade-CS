Mac n'cheese

Pour the mac and cheese out of the box into a pot of boiling water. Get the cheese mix ready by mixing the cheese mix with some milk. When the pasta is ready, pour the pasta out into a cylinder. Mix the pasta with the cheese mix and serve. Enjoy!
