Fun facts about sleep!

Physical Effects:

sleep affects stress homework mones, the immune system, appetite, blood pressure, breathing, and cardiovascular health

lack of sleep can increase risk of obesity, heart disease, and infection

Mental Effects:

sleep helps your reflexies to stay fast, thoughts to be clearer, and focus to improve

lack of sleep can cause issues with reasoning, problem solving, and attention to detail
