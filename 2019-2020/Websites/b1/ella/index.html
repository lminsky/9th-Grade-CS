CAVETOWN

For this project, I decided to create a website devoted to my favorite music artist.

Robin Skinner

AKA Robbie

is a 20-year-old singer, songwriter, and guitarist who goes by cavetown.

He has released two full albums, with other eps and special releases.

I started listening to him in the summer of 2019, when I traveled to France and Holland, and my friend introduced him to me.

After some drama in a sushi restaurant, I was able to get tickets to a concert, so now I'm going see him on November 8th, 2019.
